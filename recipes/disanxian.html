Di San Xian

Description

Disanxian a popular dish from Northern China can literally be translated as 'three treasures of the earth'. It's made using the three most common vegetables found at a Chinese Market, namely eggplant, potato and pepper.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggplants
  • 1/4 cup of corn flower for coating
  • 1 potato (cut into thick slices)
  • 1 green pepper (broadly sliced)
  • 1 green onion (chopped)
  • 2 garlic cloves (chopped)
  • oil for frying

Stir-Frying Sauce

  • 2 tbsp. light soy sauce
  • 1/4 tsp. sugar
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • 1/2 tbsp. dark soy sauce
  • 1 tsp. cornstarch
  • 3 tbsp. water
Steps
  1. soak the eggplants in slightly salted water for 15 minutes, drain, and then coat with a thin layer of cornstarch
  2. In a small bowl, mix together the stir-frying sauce ingredients
  3. Heat oil in a wok (adding more oil will expediate the process; don't worry about using too much oil, as it can be removed later)
  4. First fry the potatoes until well-done. Then add the eggplant and fry. Lastly, add and fry the green peppers for less than 1 minute
  5. Pour out the excess oil, leaving only a small amount for the last step. Add the chopped green onion and garlic until aromatic
  6. Pour in the stir-frying oil and simmer until all the ingredients are well-coated
  7. Serve with steamed rice
